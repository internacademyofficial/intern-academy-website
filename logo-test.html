Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for internships?

Simply create a student account, complete your profile, and browse our internship listings. Click 'Apply Now' on any listing that interests you.

Are the courses free?

We offer both free and premium courses. Our premium courses come with certification and mentorship support.

Can companies post internships for free?

Yes, companies can post up to 3 internships for free. For more listings and premium features, we offer enterprise plans.

Is Intern Academy available globally?

Currently, we focus on opportunities in India, but we are expanding to other regions soon.
