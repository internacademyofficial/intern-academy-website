Our Mission

At Intern Academy, we believe that every student deserves a chance to shine. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing a comprehensive internship platform connecting students with opportunities across diverse domains including Medical Fields, Pharma, Engineering, IT, Management, Marketing, Social Media, Design, Architecture, Construction, Law, and Healthcare. We partner with leading local companies to empower the next generation of professionals with real-world experience and career opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for internships?

Simply create a student account, complete your profile, and browse our internship listings. Click 'Apply Now' on any listing that interests you.

Are the courses free?

We offer both free and premium courses. Our premium courses come with certification and mentorship support.

Can companies post internships for free?

Yes, companies can post up to 3 internships for free. For more listings and premium features, we offer enterprise plans.

Is Intern Academy available globally?

We are expanding globally! Currently, we serve students and companies in India, Southeast Asia, and North America, with plans to reach more regions soon.
